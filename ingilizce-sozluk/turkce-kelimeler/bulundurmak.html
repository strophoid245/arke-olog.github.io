bulundurmak

/ı/ 1. to have or keep (something) available, at hand, or in stock: Cebinde her zaman kitap bulundurur. He always has a book in his pocket. 2. to keep, maintain (someone) in (a specified place): O mafya babası yanında sürekli iki goril bulunduruyor. That mafia chief always keeps two goons at his side.
