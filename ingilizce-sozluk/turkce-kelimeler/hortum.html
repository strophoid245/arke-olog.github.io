hortum

1. hose (for conveying fluids). 2. trunk, proboscis (of an elephant). 3. proboscis (of insects and some other invertebrates). 4. whirl-wind, waterspout. –– gibi snoutlike, snouty, snoutish (nose). –– sıkmak /a/ to jet water on (a fire) with a fire hose, stream water on (a fire) with a fire hose.
