gibisi

,-ni 1. equal: O doktor gibisi yok. That doctor has no equal. 2. the likes of, the like of: Hayatımda onun gibisini görmedim. Never in my life have I seen the likes of him. ––ne gelmek /ın/ to seem to (one), appear to (one): Olmaz gibime geliyor. It seems impossible to me. ––ne getirmek /ı/ to insinuate: Beni evinde istemiyor gibisine getirdi. He insinuated that he did not want me in his house.
