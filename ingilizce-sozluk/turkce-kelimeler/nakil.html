nakil

,-kli 1. transporting, transferring, conveying, or moving (something) from (one place) to (another). 2. moving to (another residence). 3. transferring (someone) to a new post. 4. transcribing; copying. 5. translating. 6. telling, recounting (a story). 7. med. transplanting. 8. something which one accepts on faith as being true, something which one cannot objectively or intellectually prove. –– vasıtası transport vehicle; means of transportation.
