ufak

1. small, little. 2. little, young. 3. minor, paltry, piddling. 4. crumb. –– çapta small-scale. –– para small change; small bills. –– tefek 1. minor, trifling, trivial, piddling. 2. tiny, no bigger than a minute. 3. trifles, unimportant things. –– ufak 1. a little at a time, bit by bit. 2. in small pieces.
