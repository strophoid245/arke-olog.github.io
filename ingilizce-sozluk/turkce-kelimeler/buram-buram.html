buram buram

1. (smoke´s emerging) in great clouds or puffs. 2. (something´s smelling) rankly. 3. (snow´s falling) in great whirls, in great whirling clouds. 4. (sweating) profusely, buckets. 5. (feeling or perceiving something) greatly, very much: O adamın Avrupalılığını buram buram duyarsın. You can really tell that guy´s a European.
