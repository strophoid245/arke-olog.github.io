sardırmak

/ı, a/ 1. to have (someone) wind or wrap (one thing) around (another); to have (someone) wrap (something) up in; to have (someone) wrap (something). 2. to have (someone) bandage (a wound). 3. to have (people, an army) surround (a place). 4. to have (someone) wind up or coil up (something). 5. to cause (a vine) to twine around or climb (something).
