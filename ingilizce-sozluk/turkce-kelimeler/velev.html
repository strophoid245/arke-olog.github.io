velev

1. even if .... 2. (It doesn´t matter) whether it´s (one) or whether it´s (the other): Velev ben velev sen, fark etmez. It doesn´t matter whether it´s me or whether it´s you. –– ki even if ...: Böyle birisini, velev ki dünya güzeli olsun, evime sokmam! I won´t allow such a person in my house, even if she´s the most beautiful creature on earth!
