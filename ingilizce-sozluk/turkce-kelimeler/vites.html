vites

1. (a) gear (in the transmission of a motor vehicle): birinci vites first gear/first/low gear/ low. dördüncü vites fourth gear/fourth/high gear/high. geri vites reverse gear/reverse. 2. slang penis, tool. –– büyültmek to upshift, shift to a higher gear. –– değiştirmek to shift gears. –– dişlisi change gear. –– kolu gearshift, gearshift lever, Brit. gear lever; shift stick, Brit. gear stick. –– kutusu transmission, gearbox. –– küçültmek to downshift, shift to a lower gear.
