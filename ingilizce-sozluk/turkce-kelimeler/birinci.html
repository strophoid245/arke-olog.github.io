birinci

1. first, (someone, something) who/which is first in a countable series: birinci hafta the first week. 2. first, in the first place: Koşuda birinci geldi. She came in first in the race. 3. first quality. B–– Dünya Savaşı the First World War, World War I. –– elden (buying something) new (as opposed to buying something used). –– gelmek to come first (in a competition). –– hamur best quality paper. –– mevki first class (in a train, bus), cabin class (on a ship). –– olmak to be first. –– planda olmak to have highest priority.
