mahalle

neighborhood; quarter; ward (in a city or town). ––yi ayağa kaldırmak to put the whole neighborhood in an uproar (by making noise). –– çapkını a timid and rather unsuccessful Lothario. –– çocuğu gamin, urchin, street Arab. –– kahvesi gibi crowded, stuffy, and noisy. –– karısı vulgar, quarrelsome woman; fishwife.
