kalbur

coarsely meshed sieve; riddle; screen. –– altı what has passed through a sieve; siftings; screenings. ––a çevirmek /ı/ to riddle with holes. ––a dönmek to be riddled with holes. ––dan geçirmek /ı/ to sift, sieve; to screen; to riddle. –– gibi riddled, full of holes. –– makinesi screening machine, bolting machine. ––la su taşımak to be engaged in a hopeless task.
