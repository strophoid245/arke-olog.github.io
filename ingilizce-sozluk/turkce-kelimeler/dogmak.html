doğmak

1.to be born. 2. (for the sun, the moon, or a star) to rise. 3. to appear, arise. 4. /a/ to occur (to). doğduğuna inanıp öldüğüne inanmamak colloq. to look on the bright side of something and refuse to look on its dark side. doğduğuna (bin) pişman tired of life, unhappy, miserable. doğduğuna pişman etmek /ı/ to make (someone) regret the day he was born.
