eş

1. one of a pair, mate, fellow. 2. match, equal, like, double, duplicate, counterpart. 3. husband; wife; mate, spouse. 4. mate (of an animal). 5. friend, companion. 6. prov. afterbirth, placenta. 7. partner (in a game). –– dost friends and acquaintances. ––i (benzeri) olmamak to be unique. –– tutmak /ı/ to choose (someone) as a partner (in a game).
