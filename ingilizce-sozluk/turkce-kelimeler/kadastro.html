kadastro

cadastral survey. ––sunu çıkarmak /ın/ to make a cadastral survey of (an area). –– dairesi government office where cadastral records are kept. ––ya geçmek (for property) to be registered in a cadastre. –– haritası cadastral map. –– krokisi cadastral sketch. –– memuru 1. official who surveys and appraises land. 2. official in an office for cadastral records. –– planı cadastral plan.
