yetiştirme

1. getting (someone, something) to (a place) in time. 2. having (one person) catch up with (another). 3. getting (something) done or ready in time. 4. bringing (one person) to the aid of (another) in time. 5. making (one thing) reach (another). 6. making (something) suffice. 7. raising (children, animals, plants). 8. training (animals). 9. person who´s been trained by or raised by (a specified person), protégé of; protégée of. –– yurdu orphanage, children´s home.
