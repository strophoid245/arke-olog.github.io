saklanılmak

1. to be hidden, be concealed; /dan/ (for something) to be kept secret from (someone). 2. /da/ (for something) to be kept in, be stored in (a place). 3. /a/ to be saved, kept, or set aside for (someone). 4. impersonal passive /a/ to hide something in; /dan/ to keep something secret from. 5. impersonal passive /da/ to keep or store something in. 6. impersonal passive /a/ to save something for.
