ümit

1. hope. 2. expectation. –– bağlamak /a/ to set one´s hopes on. –– dünyası. colloq. One can always hope. –– etmek /ı/ 1. to hope. 2. to expect. –– kapısı hope; person, place, or situation that may prove helpful. ––ini kesmek to abandon hope, give up hope; /dan/ to cease to hope that (someone will do something, something will happen). –– vermek /a/ to inspire (someone) with hope; to give (someone) cause to hope.
