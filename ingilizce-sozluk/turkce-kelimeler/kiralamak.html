kiralamak

1. /ı, a/ to rent, Brit. let (a house, apartment, office, etc.) (to); to rent, Brit. hire (a car, boat, horse, machine, etc.) (out) (to); to lease (something) (to). 2. /ı, dan/ to rent (a house, apartment, office, etc.) (from); to rent, Brit. hire (a car, boat, horse, machine, etc.) (from); to lease (something) (from); to charter (a boat, bus, plane, etc.) (from).
