sağlam

1. healthy, strong. 2. strong, sound, secure; well-built, well-made; in good condition, undamaged. 3. trustworthy, reliable, dependable. 4. colloq. most certainly, without a doubt. –– ayakkabı değil. colloq. He´s unreliable. (bir işi) –– kazığa/––a bağlamak to ensure that nothing goes wrong; to make (something) sure, certain, or safe. –– kaba kotarmak /ı/ to reorganize (something) so that it becomes profitable or beneficial, make (something) a going concern. –– rüzgâr steady wind.
