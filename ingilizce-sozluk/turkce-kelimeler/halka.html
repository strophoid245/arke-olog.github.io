halka

1. hoop. 2. ring, a circular band, circlet. 3. ring (for the finger, ear, or nose). 4. circle, circular ripple. 5. circle (formed by people). 6. circle (under the eye). 7. ring-shaped salty biscuit. 8. gymnastics ring. 9. slang queen, passive male homosexual. –– halka 1. in rings. 2. in links. 3. in circles. –– olmak to form a circle. –– oyunu round dance.
