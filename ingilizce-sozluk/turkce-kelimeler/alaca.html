alaca

1. multi-colored, parti-colored; motley. 2. pied; piebald; skewbald; dappled. 3. freckle, fleck (on a fruit). –– bulaca motley, parti-colored. –– düşmek /a/ (for fruit) to become freckled, become flecked: Üzümlere alaca düştü. The grapes have gotten freckled. –– karanlık (morning or evening) twilight, dusk; gloaming.
