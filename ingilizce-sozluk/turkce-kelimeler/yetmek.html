yetmek

1. to be enough, be sufficient, suffice, do. 2. /a/ to reach (a specified age): Yaşı yüze yetti. He´s reached a hundred. Yeter artık! That´s enough!/I´ve had enough!/I´m fed up! yeter ki .... All that is needed is ...; provided that ...: Bu işin üstesinden gelebilirim, yeter ki bana huzurlu bir ortam sağlansın. I can get this job done provided that I have a quiet and peaceful atmosphere in which to do it.
