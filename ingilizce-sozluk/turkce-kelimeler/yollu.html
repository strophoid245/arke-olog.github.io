yollu

1. (place) which has roads. 2. striped (cloth). 3. fast (vehicle, especially a ship). 4. done or carried out properly. 5. (something) by way of, in the nature of: öğüt yollu bir söz something said by way of advice. 6. (woman) of easy virtue. –– yolsuz işler unlawful activity, monkey business. –– yolunca properly, according to Hoyle.
