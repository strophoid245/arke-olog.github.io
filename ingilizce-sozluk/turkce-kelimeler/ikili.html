ikili

1. double, made up of two identical parts. 2. bilateral (agreement, treaty). 3. (something) which holds two measures or things. 4. duet. 5. duo. 6. (a) two (playing card or domino). –– anlaşma bilateral treaty. –– bileşik chem. binary compound. –– bisiklet tandem, tandem bicycle. –– çatı ling. syncretism. –– kapacık anat. mitral valve. –– kök gram. root used for forming both nouns and verbs. –– oynamak to play both ends against the middle. –– ünlü phonetics diphthong.
