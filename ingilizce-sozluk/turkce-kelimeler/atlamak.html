atlamak

1. to jump. 2. /dan/ to jump down (from), leap (from). 3. /a/ to jump into (a taxi, car); to catch (a plane, train). 4. journalism to miss a scoop. 5. /ı/ to omit, skip, miss, leave out. 6. /da/ to be misled, be mistaken (in). 7. /ı/ slang to give, hand over. 8. /a/ slang to have intercourse with.
