recovery (from ill health or misfortune). –– etmek /ı/ to bring about (someone´s) recovery, rehabilitate. ––ı kesilmek /ın/ colloq. to be exhausted, be dog tired. ––ını kesmek /ın/ colloq. to wear (someone) down. –– olmak to recover, get better. –– olmaz 1. incorrigible (person). 2. hopeless (situation).
