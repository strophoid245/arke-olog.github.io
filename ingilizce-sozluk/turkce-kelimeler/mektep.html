mektep

1. school. 2. slang whorehouse, cathouse. 3. slang prison, jail, the clink, the jug. ––i asmak to play hooky. ––e başlatmak /ı/ slang to sell. –– çocuğu 1. schoolboy; schoolgirl. 2. slang rank beginner, greenhorn. –– etmek /ı/ slang to sell. –– görmemiş 1. illiterate. 2. (person) who has never attended a school. 3. slang ill-mannered, rude. –– kaçağı truant, kid who is playing hooky. –– medrese görmüş educated.
