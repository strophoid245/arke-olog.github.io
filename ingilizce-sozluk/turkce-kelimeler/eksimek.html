ekşimek

1. to become tart; to sour, turn sour. 2. (for leavened dough) to rise. 3. (for one´s stomach) to get upset owing to too much acid: Midesi ekşidi. He´s got acid indigestion. 4. /başına/ slang to harass, bother (someone). 5. (for one´s face) to get a sour look on it. 6. slang to get embarrassed.
