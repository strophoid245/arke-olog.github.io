1. /dan, a/ to be brought from (a place) to (someone or a place). 2. /a/ to be brought to (someone or a place). 3. to be fetched. 4. to be put forward, be brought forth, be set forth, be presented. 5. /a/ to be appointed to (a position, an office, etc.), be brought to (a position, an office, etc.), be designated (a title).
