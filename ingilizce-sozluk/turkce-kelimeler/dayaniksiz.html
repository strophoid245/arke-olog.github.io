dayanıksız

1. not durable, undurable; not sturdy, frail. 2. (someone) who lacks physical or mental endurance, who lacks stamina, who lacks staying power, weak, frail. 3. /a/ not resistant to. –– mallar com. nondurable goods, nondurables. –– tüketim malları com. nondurable consumer goods, consumer nondurables.
