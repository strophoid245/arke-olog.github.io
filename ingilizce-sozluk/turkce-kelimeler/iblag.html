iblağ

1. communicating, conveying, or reporting (something) (to). 2. raising or increasing (an amount) to. –– etmek /ı, a/ 1. to communicate, convey, or report (something) (to). 2. to raise (an amount) to, increase (an amount) to: Hasan sermayesini yirmi milyar liraya iblağ etmek istiyor. Hasan wants to increase his capital to twenty billion liras.
