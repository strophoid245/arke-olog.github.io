için için

1. secretly, covertly. 2. internally, inwardly. –– ağlamak to weep inwardly. –– gülmek to laugh up one´s sleeve, laugh inwardly, be secretly amused. –– yanmak 1. to burn internally. 2. to burn slowly and silently. 3. inwardly to burn with passion. 4. inwardly to be deeply distressed; secretly to feel very sad.
