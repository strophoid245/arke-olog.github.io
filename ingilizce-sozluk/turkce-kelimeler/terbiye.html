terbiye

1. teaching (someone) good manners; disciplining. 2. good manners, manners. 3. training, education (of a person). 4. taming or training (of an animal). 5. training (a voice). 6. any of several sauces made primarily with lemon juice and whole eggs. –– almak 1. to be taught (good) manners. 2. to be trained, be educated. ––sini bozmak to do something unmannerly, forget one´s manners, be rude. –– etmek /ı/ 1. to teach (someone) good manners; to discipline (someone). 2. to train, educate (someone). 3. to train or tame (an animal). 4. to train (a voice). –– görmek 1. to be taught (good) manners. 2. to be trained, be educated. ––sini takınmak to mind one´s manners; to begin to behave in a mannerly way. ––sini vermek /ın/ to give (someone) a dressing down.
