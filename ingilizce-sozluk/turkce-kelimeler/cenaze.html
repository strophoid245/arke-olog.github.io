cenaze

1. corpse which has been made ready for burial, body ready for burial. 2. funeral, obsequies. –– alayı 1. those in attendance at a funeral, mourners (usually used only of a large number of people). 2. funeral, obsequies. –– arabası hearse. –– gibi 1. (someone) who looks like death warmed over, who is very wan and emaciated. 2. (someone) who is as pale as a corpse. ––yi kaldırmak to hold a funeral. –– kalkmak /dan/ for a funeral to be held at (a specified place). –– levazımatçısı undertaker. –– marşı funeral march. –– namazı Islam prayer performed at a funeral. –– salası Islam funeral prayer (recited from the minaret). –– töreni funeral ceremony, funeral, obsequies.
