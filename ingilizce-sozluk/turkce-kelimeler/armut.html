armut

1. pear. 2. slang fool, imbecile, blockhead. ––un/ahlatın iyisini (dağda) ayılar yer. proverb The undeserving are often lucky. –– piş, ağzıma düş. colloq. He expects things to fall into his lap without doing anything about it himself. ––un sapı var, üzümün/kirazın çöpü var demek to find a fault in everything.
