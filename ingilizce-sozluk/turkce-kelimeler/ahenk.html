ahenk

1. mus. harmony. 2. musicality. 3. harmony, integration, accord, agreement, compatibility. 4. lively party at which musicians provide entertainment. ––ini bozmak /ın/ to create disunity between or in, upset the tranquillity that existed between or in. –– kaidesi phonetics vowel harmony. –– kurmak /da/ to establish harmonious relations (between members of a group). –– sağlamak /da/ to secure unity and order (in a place). –– tahtası soundboard, belly, table (of a violin, a lute, or a similar instrument).
