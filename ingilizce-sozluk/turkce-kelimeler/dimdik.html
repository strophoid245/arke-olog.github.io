dimdik

1. straight as a rod, bolt upright, very erect. 2. straight up. 3. very steep; precipitous. 4. (looking) fixedly, penetratingly, intently. 5. (standing up) well; with one´s head up. 6. directly, straight ahead, straight on. –– ayakta durmak not to have been destroyed, to survive intact. –– durmak 1. to stand erect. 2. to hold fast to one´s point of view.
