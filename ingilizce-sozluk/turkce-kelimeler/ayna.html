ayna

1. mirror, looking glass. 2. telescope. 3. sextant. 4. panel (of a door or wainscot). 5. blade (of an oar). 6. smooth water (over an upswell). 7. kneecap (of a horse). 8. slang perfect, going very well. 9. curtain (in a shadow show). –– gibi 1. mirror-like, lustrous, clean, bright. 2. glassy, without a ripple. –– tırnağı metal piece used to hold a mirror on the wall.
