kaynak

1. fountainhead, source (of a stream or river); spring. 2. source, origin. 3. written source (of information). 4. weld, welded place. 5. patch, patched place (on rubber). 6. welding. 7. patching (rubber). –– suyu spring water. –– tozu welding flux. –– yapmak /a/ 1. to weld. 2. to patch (rubber).
