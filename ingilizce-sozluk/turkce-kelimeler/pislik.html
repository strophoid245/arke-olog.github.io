pislik

1. dirt, filth. 2. dirtiness, filthiness. 3. (an) obscenity; (a) profanity. 4. excrement, feces. 5. beastliness, nastiness, vileness. 6. vile, nasty, or low-down action. –– götürmek /ı/ (for a place) to be very dirty: Evi pislik götürüyor. The house is as dirty as a pigsty. –– paçasından/paçalarından akmak (for someone) to be very dirty, be covered with dirt.
