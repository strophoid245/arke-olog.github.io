terazi

1. balance, scales, pair of scales, scale. 2. pole (used by a tightrope walker to balance himself). –– dili pointer of a balance. –– eli handle of a balance. –– gözü pan or scale of a balance. –– kolu beam or lever (supported by a fulcrum and forming the two arms of a balance). –– tablası platform (of an automatic scale). ––ye vurmak /ı/ to think about (something) carefully, weigh (something) carefully in one´s mind.
