sakınmak

1. /dan/ to avoid (something, someone); to keep away from, steer clear of (someone). 2. /dan/ to guard against; to watch out for (something dangerous). 3. /ı, dan/ to protect (something, someone) (from). Sakınılan göze çöp batar. proverb The thing that one is most anxious to keep out of harm´s way is the thing that always ends up getting damaged.
