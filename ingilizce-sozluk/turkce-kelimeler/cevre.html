çevre

1. surroundings, environs; vicinity, neighborhood: Çankırı çevresinde in the vicinity of Çankırı. 2. perimeter, encompassing limits, periphery, circumference. 3. natural environment, environment; habitat. 4. (a person´s) circle of friends and acquaintances. 5. circle, group of people who share a common interest or pursuit. 6. embroidered handkerchief. Ç–– Bakanı Minister for the Environment.
