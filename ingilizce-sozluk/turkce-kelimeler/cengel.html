çengel

1. hook. 2. Turkish wrestling hooking one´s foot around the leg of one´s opponent. –– atışı basketball hook shot. –– burun hook nose. –– pas basketball hook pass. –– şut basketball hook shot. –– takmak /a/ to pester. ––e vurmak hist. to impale (someone) on a hook (as a punishment for a crime). –– vuruş boxing hook.
