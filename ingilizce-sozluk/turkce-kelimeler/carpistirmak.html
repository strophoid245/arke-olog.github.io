çarpıştırmak

1. /ı/ to hit, bang, or strike (two things together); /ı, la/ to knock, hit, bang, or strike (one thing) against (another). 2. /ı/ to make or allow (two military forces) to fight, skirmish, or join battle; /ı, la/ to make or allow (one military force) to fight, skirmish, or battle with (another).
