hırsız

thief, burglar, robber. –– çetesi gang of thieves. –– feneri dark lantern. –– gibi furtively, surreptitiously, stealthily. ––a kilit/kapı/baca olmaz. proverb 1. There is no real safeguard against burglars. 2. If someone is really bent on evil there is no way to stop him. –– yatağı 1. den of thieves. 2. hiding place for stolen goods, hide, cache. 3. receiver of stolen goods, fence. ––a yol göstermek inadvertently to help a wrongdoer.
