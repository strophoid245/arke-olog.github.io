kurmak

/ı/ 1. to set up, assemble, put together. 2. to set (the table) (for a meal). 3. to pitch (a tent). 4. to wind (a clock, watch). 5. to cock (a gun). 6. to set (a trap). 7. to prepare (a mixture) and set it aside to pickle or ferment. 8. to establish, found; to form, create. 9. to plot, plan (something bad). 10 to indulge in (daydreams). 11. to resolve (to do something). 12. to ponder, dwell on. 13. to set (one person) against another. kurup takma assembling, putting (something) together. kurup takmak /ı/ to assemble, fit/put together. kurduğu tuzağa kendi düşmek to fall into one´s own trap.
