bağlatmak

1. /a, ı/ to have or let (someone) tie (someone, something) up; /ı, a/ to have (someone, something) tied or bound to. 2. /a, ı/ to have (someone) tie (shoes, shoelaces, a ribbon, a tie). 3. /a, ı/ to have (someone) bandage (a wound). 4. /a, ı/ to have (someone) tie (something) up in a bundle. 5. /a, ı/ to have (someone) put on (a salary); to have (a regular supply of rations) assigned to (someone). 6. /a, ı/ to have (someone) finalize (a business deal). 7. /ı, a/ to cause (someone) to become devoted to.
