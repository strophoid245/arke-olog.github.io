tahakkuk

,-ku 1. becoming a reality, realization. 2. turning out to be true. 3. accrual, accruement (of interest, taxes, etc.); falling due (of a tax), interest. –– etmek 1. to become a reality, be realized. 2. to turn out to be true, prove true. 3. (for interest, a tax) to accrue; (for a tax, interest) to fall due. –– memuru tax assessor. –– tarihi due date (of a tax, interest).
