sanki

1. as if: Sanki dünyanın sonu gelmiş gibi konuşuyor. He´s talking as if it were the end of the world. 2. It´s as if ...: Sanki hiç olmamış. It´s as if it had never taken place. 3. Do you really think ...?/You don´t really think ..., do you?: Taksiye de binsen sanki yetişebilir miydin? Hadi canım sen de! Do you really mean to say you think you could have got there in time if you´d taken a taxi? Tell me another one!
