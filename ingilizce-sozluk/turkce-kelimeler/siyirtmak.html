sıyırtmak

1. /ı, a/ to have (someone) strip or skim (something) off. 2. /ı, a/ to have (someone) draw (a sword). 3. /ı, a/ to have (someone) scour or scrape (something) clean; to have (someone) pick (a bone) clean; to have (someone) eat every morsel of food on (his plate). 4. /ı, a/ to have (someone) tear (a cloth) into strips. 5. /ı, dan/ to enable (someone) to get out of (a jam).
