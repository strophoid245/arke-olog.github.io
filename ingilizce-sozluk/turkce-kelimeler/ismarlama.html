ısmarlama

1. /ı or a, ı/ telling (someone) to bring (something); ordering (something); requesting (someone) to bring (something), asking (someone) to bring or get (something). 2. /ı, a/ having (someone) make (something) to order. 3. /a, ı/ treating (someone) to (something), buying (someone) (something). 4. /ı, a/ entrusting (someone, something) to (someone). 5. /a/ warning (someone) not to do something; telling (someone) to do something. 6. made-to-order, custom-made. 7. tailor-made (article of clothing). 8. forced, artificial, put-on. 9. slapdash, done in a slapdash way, slipshod. –– elbise (man´s) tailor-made suit; (woman´s) tailor-made dress.
