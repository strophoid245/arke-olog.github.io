başıboş

1. unconfined or untethered (animal); (child) who is allowed to roam about by himself/herself, unsupervised. 2. unfettered, untrammeled. 3. aimlessly, at will. –– bırakmak /ı/ 1. to let (an animal, a child) run loose. 2. to let (someone) do as he/she pleases. –– kalmak 1. to be left unconfined or unsupervised. 2. to be left to do as one pleases.
