içmek

1. /ı/ to drink, imbibe, consume (something) by drinking it. 2. /ı/ to smoke. 3. /ı/ to eat (soup). 4. /ı/ (for something) to absorb, drink, drink up (fluid): Toprak suyun hepsini içti. The soil absorbed all of the water. 5. to drink alcoholic beverages, drink, imbibe. içtikleri su ayrı gitmemek to be very close friends.
