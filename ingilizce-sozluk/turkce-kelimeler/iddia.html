iddia

1. claim, assertion; allegation. 2. pretension; pretense. 3. obstinacy. –– etmek 1. /ı/ to claim, assert; to allege. 2. /ı/ to pretend. 3. to be obstinate, insist obstinately. ––ya girişmek/girmek/tutuşmak /ına/ to make a bet with each other (about). –– makamı the office or rank of a district attorney.
