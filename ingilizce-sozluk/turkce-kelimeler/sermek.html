sermek

1. /ı, a/ to spread (something) out on (the ground or floor); to spread (something) over; to spread (something) out in (the sun). 2. to hang (something) up on (a line). 3. to send (someone) sprawling, flatten (someone). 4. /ı/ to neglect (something). sere serpe 1. (stretched out) at full length, comfortably. 2. (moving around) freely, comfortably.
