yüklü

1. loaded; freighted; loaded or freighted with: kömür yüklü bir kamyon a truck loaded with coal. 2. (someone) who´s been given a lot of work to do; (someone) who has a lot of work to do. 3. full, demanding (schedule of activities). 4. pregnant. 5. slang drunk, loaded, tanked. 6. slang rich, loaded. –– bir para colloq. a lot of money, a big sum of money.
