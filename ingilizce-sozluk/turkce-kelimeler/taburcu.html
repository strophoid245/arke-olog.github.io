taburcu

1. discharged or dismissed (from a hospital). 2. slang released from jail. 3. slang fired from one´s job. 4. person who´s been discharged from a hospital, released from jail, or fired. –– etmek /ı/ 1. to discharge, dismiss (a hospital patient). 2. slang to release (a prisoner). 3. slang to fire (someone). –– olmak 1. (for a hospital patient) to be discharged, be dismissed. 2. slang (for a prisoner) to be released. 3. slang to get fired.
