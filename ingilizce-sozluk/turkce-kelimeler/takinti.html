takıntı

1. thing that is connected with or related to something else, ramification: O meseleyi bütün takıntılarıyla konuştuk. We discussed that matter and the things related to it. 2. outstanding debt; outstanding obligation. 3. person (for whom one is obliged to do something). 4. relationship, relations, dealings (with someone). 5. subject which a student has flunked. 6. prov. piece of jewelry.
