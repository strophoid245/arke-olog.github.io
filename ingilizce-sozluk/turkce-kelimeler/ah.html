ah

1. Ah!/Oh!/Alas! 2. sigh, groan. 3. lament. 4. curse. –– almak to be cursed for one´s cruelty. –– çekmek to sigh. ––ını çekmek /ın/ to be under the curses of (those one has wronged). ––ı çıkmak /ın/ to suffer as a result of the curse of (someone whom one has wronged). –– etmek 1. to sigh. 2. /a/ to curse. ––ı tutmak/yerde kalmamak to have one´s curse take effect.
