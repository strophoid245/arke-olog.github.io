kir

dirt, filth. –– fitili roll of dirt or dead skin (rubbed from the body). –– götürmek/kaldırmak not to show dirt. –– götürmek /ı/ to be filthy, be covered with dirt. ––i kabarmak for the dirt in (one´s) skin to come to the surface (when the skin is rubbed or scratched). –– tutmak to show dirt easily, get dirty quickly.
