deri

1. skin, hide. 2. leather. 3. peel, rind. –– bağlamak (for a wound, sore, etc.) to heal, close up. –– hastalığı skin disease. –– kaplı leather-bound (book). ––si kemiklerine yapışmak to be just skin and bones, be skinny. ––sine sığmaz conceited, stuck-up. –– yangısı path. dermatitis. ––sini yüzmek /ın/ 1. to flay, skin. 2. to rob (someone) blind. 3. to torture (someone) to death.
