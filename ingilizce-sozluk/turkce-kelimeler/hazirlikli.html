hazırlıklı

1. (someone) whose preparations have been completed, prepared, ready: Biz henüz hazırlıklı değiliz. Our preparations have not yet finished. 2. prepared in advance: hazırlıklı bir nutuk a speech which was prepared in advance. 3. after having made preparations in advance: Ali her zaman hazırlıklı konuşur. Ali always gives speeches that he´s prepared in advance.
