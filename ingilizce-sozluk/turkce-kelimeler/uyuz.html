uyuz

1. the itch, scabies; mange (in dogs); scab (in cattle). 2. itchy; scabious, scabietic; mangy; scabby. 3. ratty-looking, mangy-looking, moth-eaten. 4. slang penniless, flat broke. 5. slang incompetent. –– olmak 1. to get the itch, scabies, the mange, or scab. 2. slang to become penniless, go broke, go bust. 3. slang to get very angry, blow one´s stack.
