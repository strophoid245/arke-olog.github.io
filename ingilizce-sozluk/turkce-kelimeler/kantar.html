kantar

1. steelyard, lever scales. 2. scales (for vehicles), weighbridge. 3. formerly a weight of about 56.5 kilos, kantar, cantar. ––ı belinde colloq. alert, not to be fooled. ––a çekmek/vurmak /ı/ 1. to weigh (with a steelyard). 2. to weigh in one´s mind. ––a gelmek to be weighable. ––ın topunu kaçırmak to overstep the limit, go to extremes.
