fayrap

naut. 1. Fire her up! 2. Stoke her up! –– etmek 1. /ı/ to light, fire (the furnace, boiler, stove, etc.); to stoke up, fire up (the furnace, boiler, stove, etc.). 2. /ı/ slang to get a move on with, get cracking on (a job). 3. /ı/ slang to open (a window or door) all the way. 4. /ı/ slang to take off, strip off (a piece of clothing). 5. sailor´s slang to hurry up.
