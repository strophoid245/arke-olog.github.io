içirilmek

1. /a/ to be made to drink; to be allowed to drink: Mesude´ye çok ilaç içirildi. Mesude was made to drink a lot of medicine. 2. colloq. to be made to drink oneself drunk. 3. /a/ to be made to smoke; to be allowed to smoke: Şemsi´ye hiç sigara içirilmedi. Şemsi´s never been allowed to smoke. 4. /a/ to be made to absorb.
