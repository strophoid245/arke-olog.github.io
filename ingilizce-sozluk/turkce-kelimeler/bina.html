bina

1. (a) building; edifice; structure, (a) construction: Yüksek binaları sevmez. He doesn´t like tall buildings. 2. building, constructing, erecting (a structure). 3. basing (a statement, a case) on (something). –– etmek 1. /ı/ to build, construct, erect. 2. /ı, a/ to base (a statement, a case) on. –– vergisi tax on buildings.
