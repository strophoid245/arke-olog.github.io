endaze

1. measure; proportion. 2. obs. a measure of length of about 25 inches, 65 cm. ––sini almak /ın/ to estimate the ability of (someone); to estimate the importance and consequences of (a matter). ––si bozuk ill-proportioned; not in proper order. ––ye gelmemek to be inestimable, be measureless, be immeasurable. ––ye vurmak /ı/ to measure; to calculate, consider.
