çözülmek

1. to be untied; to be unfastened; to be undone; to be unraveled; to be disentangled. 2. to be solved. 3. to begin to thaw. 4. to disintegrate, dissolve. 5. mil. (for an army) to begin to withdraw from the field of battle. 6. (for one´s hand, foot, arm, leg) to become weak. 7. slang to run away, beat it. 8. slang to tell what one knows, squeal.
