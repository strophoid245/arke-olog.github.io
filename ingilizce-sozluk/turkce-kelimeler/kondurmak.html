kondurmak

1. /ı, a/ to make (a bird, insect) alight upon. 2. /ı, a/ to tack on, stick on. 3. /ı, a/ to accept (that one is ...): Hastalığı kendine kondurmuyor. He won´t accept that he´s ill. 4. /ı, a/ to label (someone) a ..., put (someone) down as a ...: Bönlüğü ona kondurdu. He put him down as a simpleton. 5. /ı, a/ suddenly to land (a blow) or place (a kiss) on. 6. /ı/ to give (a quick reply).
