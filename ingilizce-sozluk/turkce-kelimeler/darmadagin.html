darmadağın

,darmadağınık 1. (place, thing) which is in complete disarray. 2. (things, people) which/who have scattered everywhere. 3. very tousled (hair). 4. (mind) which can´t concentrate on anything. –– etmek /ı/ 1. to disorder, disarray, or mess up completely. 2. to scatter everywhere. 3. to confuse utterly.
