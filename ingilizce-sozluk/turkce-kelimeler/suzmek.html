süzmek

/ı/ 1. to strain; to filter; to strain (the residue) out of (a liquid): Sirkenin tortusunu süzdü. He strained the sediment out of the vinegar. 2. to give (someone) the once-over, give (someone) an appraising glance. 3. to lower (one´s eyelids) languorously: Gözlerini süzdü. She let her eyelids droop languorously.
