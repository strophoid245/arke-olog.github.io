soluk

breath. bir ––ta in a flash, in a trice. –– aldırmamak /a/ not to give (someone) time to catch his breath, to work (someone) very hard. –– alma physiol. inhalation, inspiration. –– almak 1. to breathe; to inhale. 2. to take a breather, rest. ––u (bir yerde) almak to find oneself in (a place) in no time flat; to reach (a place) in no time flat. –– borusu anat. windpipe, trachea. –– borusu açımı surg. tracheotomy. –– darlığı dyspnea. –– durması apnea. ––u kesilmek 1. to get out of breath. 2. to lose one´s energy or inspiration; to become burned out, lose one´s vital sparks. –– soluğa 1. panting for breath, out of breath. 2. in agitated haste. –– verme physiol. exhalation, expiration. –– vermek to exhale.
