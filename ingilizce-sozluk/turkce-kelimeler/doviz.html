döviz

1. foreign money; foreign exchange, bills redeemable in foreign money. 2. motto, slogan, or greeting inscribed on a decorative ribbon or poster. 3. placard. 4. slang money. ––e çevrilebilir mevduat deposits convertible into foreign currency. –– kaçakçılığı smuggling of foreign exchange. –– kaçırmak to smuggle foreign exchange.
