tulum

1. animal skin (used as a casing or receptacle). 2. overalls; jump suit. 3. tube (for toothpaste, medicine, etc.). 4. bagpipe. –– çıkarmak 1. to skin an animal. 2. (for one party or group) to win all the seats in an election. –– çıkmak to achieve exactly what one had set out to achieve. –– gibi 1. swollen all over. 2. as fat as a pig. –– peyniri cheese encased in a skin.
