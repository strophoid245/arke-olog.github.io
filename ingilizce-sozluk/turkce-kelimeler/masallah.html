maşallah

1. Wonderful!/Magnificent!/Just look at that! 2. May God preserve him/her from evil! 3. Wonders never cease! (said to indicate surprise). 4. amulet bearing the inscription Maşallah in Arabic characters (pinned to children to ward off the evil eye). ––ı var. /ın/ colloq., used to show admiration: Oğlunuzun maşallahı var. What a fine lad your son is! Arabasının maşallahı var. His car is really dandy!
