bey

1. gentleman, sir; a title meaning Mr. (used after a first name): Mehmet Bey. 2. prince, ruler, chieftain; chief, head, master. 3. notable, country gentleman. 4. playing cards ace. –– armudu a large variety of pear. –– baba father (used also for other respected elderly persons). –– gibi yaşamak to live the life of Riley, be comfortable, be well-off.
