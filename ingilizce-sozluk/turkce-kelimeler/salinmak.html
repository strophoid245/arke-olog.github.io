salınmak

1. to sway; to oscillate. 2. /a/ to be sent to, be dispatched to. 3. /a/ (for an animal) to be put out to pasture in (a place); (for something) to be channeled or directed into (a place). 4. /a/ (for something) to be lowered into (a place). 5. /a/ (for something) to be added to (a food). salına salına yürümek to walk with a swaying movement.
