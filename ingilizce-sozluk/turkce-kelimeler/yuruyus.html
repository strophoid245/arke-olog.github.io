yürüyüş

1. walking; way of walking, gait. 2. marching; way of marching. 3. walk (done for exercise). 4. march (especially one organized to protest or proclaim something). ––e çıkmak to go out for a walk. –– yapmak 1. to go on a walk. 2. to hold a protest march; to hold a march to proclaim something.
