yerli

1. local; indigenous, native; autochthonous; aboriginal. 2. locally produced, locally made, local. 3. domestic (as opposed to foreign). 4. immovable, not portable, built-in (piece of furniture). 5. person who is a native of a place, native, local; autochthon, aborigine. –– yerinde in its proper place; in place, in situ, in its original position. –– yerine into its proper place. –– yersiz (doing something) without stopping to consider whether or not it´s appropriate.
