düz

1. smooth, even; flat, level. 2. straight. 3. plain, simple; plain-colored, without ornament. 4. plain, level area. –– arazi level land. ––e çıkmak to get through a difficult situation. –– duvara tırmanmak to be very naughty. –– nefes etmek /ı/ slang to overcome, get the better of (someone). –– uçuş level flight. –– ünlü phonetics unrounded vowel.
