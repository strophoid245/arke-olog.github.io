kuyu

1. well. 2. pit; deep hole. 3. (mine) shaft. –– açmak to dig a well; to dig a pit. –– bileziği wellcurb. –– fındığı a hazelnut that has been picked green and then buried for a time so that it will acquire a special flavor. –– gibi 1. very deep. 2. (place) that resembles a dark hole. ––sunu kazmak /ın/ to lay a trap for (someone) to walk into. –– kebabı meat put in an earthen pot and roasted in a hole in the earth.
