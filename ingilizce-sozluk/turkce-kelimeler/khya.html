kâhya

1. hist. steward, majordomo. 2. person who is in charge of the queue at a shared-taxi stand. 3. person who looks after parked cars; parking lot attendant. 4. busybody. –– kadın formerly 1. chief female servant in a harem. 2. housekeeper. (başına) –– kesilmek /ın/ to begin to stick one´s nose into (someone else´s) business.
