dem

1. breath. 2. moment; time. 3. (alcoholic) drink. 4. condition of well-steeped tea. 5. well-steeped tea. 6. slang dope, hashish. –– çekmek 1. (for a nightingale, turtledove, etc.) to sing long and sweetly. 2. colloq. to drink, booze. –– tutmak /a/ to accompany (a piece of music). –– vurmak /dan/ to talk about (something) at random.
