salma

1. release, setting (someone, something) free. 2. putting out (roots, shoots). 3. sending, dispatching. 4. turning (an animal) out to graze. 5. a stew containing rice. 6. hist. a policeman. 7. head tax (levied in a village). 8. prov. a sleeve. 9. aviary; cote, loft. 10. (animal) which has been let out to graze. 11. (water) that is not dammed up or stagnant, continuously running (water). –– gezmek to wander around idly.
