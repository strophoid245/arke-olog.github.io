fakir

1. poor, destitute, needy, impoverished. 2. poor, mean, run-down, shoddy (thing). 3. poor person; pauper. 4. (an) unfortunate. 5. your humble servant (a polite formula now used jocularly). 6. dervish, sufi (an Indian) fakir. F––! Poor man!/Poor woman!/Poor kid!/Poor thing! –– düşmek to become poor. –– fukara the poor.
