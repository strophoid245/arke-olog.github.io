geçirtmek

1. /a, ı/ to have (someone) pass (something) through/over. 2. /ı, dan/ to have (someone) cross or traverse (a place). 3. /ı, dan/ to have (someone, something) passed through/over. 4. /ı, a/ to have (something) moved or carried over to (a place). 5. /ı, a/ to have (something) recorded on/in, have (something) written down on/in. 6. /a, ı/ to have (glass) put, fit, fixed, or inserted into (a frame). 7. /a, ı/ to have (a cover, a case) put or slipped on/over.
