hey

1. Look here!/Hey! 2. Hey, ...!/O ...!/Ah, ...! (especially used in exclamations of regret or reproach): Hey eski günler! O for the good old days! Hey budala! Hey, you idiot! Hey koca müfettiş hey! O you great inspector, you! –– gidi used when nostalgically referring to the past: Hey gidi gençlik! O for the days of youth! –– gidi hey! O for those days!
