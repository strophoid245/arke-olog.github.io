apışmak

1. (for a tired animal) to sink down with its legs splayed out; /a/ to splay itself out on (the ground, floor, etc.). 2. to squat down on one´s heels with one´s legs apart. apışıp kalmak to be dumbfounded, be left open-mouthed; to be at a loss to know what to do, be bumfuzzled; to be completely overawed.
