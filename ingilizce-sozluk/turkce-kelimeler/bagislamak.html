bağışlamak

1. /ı, a/ to donate (something) to; to grant (something) to. 2. /ı/ to pardon (someone); to forgive (a misdeed, a debt). 3. /ı/ Christianity to absolve, pardon. 4. /ı/ to spare, refrain from killing (someone); to spare (someone´s life). 5. /ı/ to excuse, forgive (someone) (for a small fault).
