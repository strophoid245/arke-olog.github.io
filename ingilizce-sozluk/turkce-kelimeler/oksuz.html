öksüz

1. orphan (child); half-orphan; fatherless; motherless. 2. (an) orphan; (a) fatherless child; (a) motherless child. 3. (someone) who is alone in the world, who has no friends or relatives. ––ler anası/babası a person who takes care of the poor and the homeless. –– sevindiren cheap, but gaudy thing.
