paça

1. trotter (especially that of a sheep). 2. jellied trotter. 3. lower part of a trouser leg. ––larından akmak to be filthy, be very dirty. ––sını çekecek/toplayacak hali olmamak to be hopelessly weak or incompetent. ––sı düşük untidy-looking, slovenly looking, down-at-heels. ––sını kaptırmak /a/ to get involved in (a business) and not be able to get out of it. –– kasnağı a wrestling maneuver. ––yı kurtarmak to extricate oneself from a difficult situation. ––ları sıvamak to get ready to start some important business, roll up one´s sleeves. ––sından tutup atmak /ı/ to get rid of (someone) (by insulting him).
