koyun

,-ynu 1. bosom, breast, 2. arms, embrace. ––una almak /ı/ to take (someone) to bed with one. ––una girmek /ın/ to go to bed with (someone). ––una koymak /ı/ to put into one´s bosom or breast pocket. –– koyuna 1. in each other´s arms. 2. in the same bed. ––unda yılan beslemek to nourish a viper in one´s bosom.
