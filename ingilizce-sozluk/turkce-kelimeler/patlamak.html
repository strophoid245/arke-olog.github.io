patlamak

1. to burst, explode, blow up. 2. to split open; to burst open. 3. (for something unpleasant) to break out. 4. /dan/ to be ready to explode or feel like screaming owing to (anger or boredom). 5. to explode, give vent to one´s feelings. 6. /a, a/ colloq. (for something) to cost (someone) (so much): Bu daire ona beş yüz milyona patladı. This apartment cost him five hundred million. Patlama! colloq. Just hold your horses a minute!/Don´t blow your stack!
