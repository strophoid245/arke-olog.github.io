kibarlık

1. breeding, refinement. 2. polite word or action. (üstünden, paçalarından) –– akmak to be overly refined, be pretentiously polite. –– budalası overly/pretentiously polite person; unrefined person who tries to act refined. –– düşkünü overly polite, stuffily polite. –– etmek to do a polite thing, behave politely. –– taslamak to pretend to be refined.
