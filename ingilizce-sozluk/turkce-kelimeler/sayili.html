sayılı

1. counted, numbered. 2. bearing (a certain) number: 8 sayılı kart the card bearing the number eight. 3. limited in number; in limited supply; few and far between. 4. (one of) a very few (persons, things): Onu görmüş olan sayılı kişilerden biridir. She´s one of the very few who saw him. 5. best, topnotch: O kadın sayılı yazarlarımızdan biridir. She´s one of our best writers. 6. special, exceptional. –– gün/günler çabuk/tez geçer. proverb Deadlines come to an end in no time flat.
