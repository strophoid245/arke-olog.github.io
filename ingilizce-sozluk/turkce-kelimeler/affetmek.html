affetmek

1. /ı/ to forgive (an act). 2. /ı/ to excuse (a person). 3. /ı/ to pardon (a convict). 4. /ı/ to excuse, give leave. 5. /ı, dan/ to dismiss, discharge (someone) from (his job or a duty). Affedersiniz. I beg your pardon./Excuse me./I´m sorry. Affetmişsin sen onu! colloq. Excuse me but you are wrong! Affetmişsiniz .... colloq. Excuse me but you are wrong.: Affetmişsiniz, öyle bir şey demedim. Excuse me but I didn´t say that.
