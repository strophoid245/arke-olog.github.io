ellilik

1. a unit of value which has a denomination of fifty (e.g. a coin or a stamp worth fifty liras). 2. (a) quinquagenarian. 3. (something) which is priced at or worth fifty units of a currency. 4. (something) which is fifty units in length, weight, volume, etc. 5. (something) which holds fifty units or measures. 6. quinquagenarian, (someone) who is in his/her fifties.
