yuvarlatmak

/ı, a/ 1. to have (someone) roll (something) (along a surface). 2. to have (someone) roll (something) up. 3. to have (someone) round (something), have (someone) give (something) a circular or spherical shape. 4. to have (someone) send (someone) sprawling (to the ground). 5. to have (someone) down (food, drink) quickly; to have (someone) put away, pack away, or polish off (food) quickly.
