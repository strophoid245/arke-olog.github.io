güm

1. boom, a booming sound; bang. 2. a loud thump. 3. slang hot air, guff, baloney, bull, bullshit. –– atmak slang to talk hot air, talk guff, talk baloney, throw bull, bullshit. –– etmek 1. to boom, make a booming sound; to bang. 2. to make a loud thump. ––e gitmek colloq. 1. (for one´s words) to fall on deaf ears; (for something) to be expended for nothing. 2. (for someone) to die needlessly. –– güm 1. with repeated booms; with bangs. 2. with loud thumps. –– güm atmak (for one´s heart) to thump violently. –– güm etmek 1. to boom repeatedly; to bang repeatedly. 2. to thump loudly.
