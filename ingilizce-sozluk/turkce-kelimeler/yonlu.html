yönlü

1. (matter, problem) which has (a specified number of) sides or aspects: çok yönlü bir soru a many-sided question. 2. math. directed (line segment, vector). 3. (road) on which traffic may proceed in (one or two) directions: tek yönlü sokak a one-way street. iki yönlü sokak a two-way street. 4. (someone) who has (many) abilities or talents. çok yönlü bir adam a man of parts.
