kastetmek

1. /ı/ to mean to say, mean: Maruf demekle neyi kastediyorsunuz? What do you mean by saying “famous”? Köy deyince Dudullu´yu kastediyor? When he says “the village” he means Dudullu. Beni mi kastetti? Did she mean me? 2. /ı/ to intend, purpose, aim, mean: Bununla ona bir ders vermeyi kastettiler. They intended this to be a lesson to him. 3. /a/ to harbor evil intentions towards, have designs upon, intend to do (someone) harm.
