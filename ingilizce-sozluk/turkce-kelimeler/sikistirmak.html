sıkıştırmak

1. /ı/ to tighten; to compress. 2. /ı/ to wedge in; to jam in; to squeeze (people, things) in, crowd (people, things) in. 3. /ı/ to press, pressure, put pressure on (someone). 4. /ı, a/ to slip (something) quietly into (someone´s hand, pocket, etc.). 5. /ı/ to squeeze or pinch (someone). 6. /ı/ to corner (a fleeing person). 7. /ı, a/ to catch, pinch (one´s finger, etc.) in (a place).
