sıfır

1. zero. 2. (a) nothing, completely, worthless person or thing. ––dan başlamak to start from scratch, start from square one. –– numara slang excellent, super. –– numara tıraş shaving of the head. ––a sıfır, elde var sıfır. colloq. All this work has been for nothing. ––ı tüketmek slang 1. to be left with absolutely nothing. 2. to go for broke (as a matter of necessity). 3. for one´s patience to come to an end. 4. to die, croak, kick the bucket, give up the ghost.
