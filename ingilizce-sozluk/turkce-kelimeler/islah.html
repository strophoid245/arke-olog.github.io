ıslah

1. improvement; betterment; amelioration; melioration. 2. correction; reform. –– etmek /ı/ 1. to improve, remove the defects or drawbacks (of). 2. to reform, discipline. 3. to retrain (a horse that´s been badly trained). –– istasyonu breeding station (for plants or animals). –– olmak to reform, change one´s ways. –– olmaz incorrigible.
