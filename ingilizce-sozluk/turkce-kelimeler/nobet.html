nöbet

,-ti 1. turn (of duty). 2. shift (scheduled period of work); watch (of a sentry). 3. time: iki nöbet twice. 4. attack, fit, paroxysm, or seizure (caused by a recurrent disease). –– beklemek 1. to await one´s turn. 2. to be on duty. 3. to stand guard; to keep watch, watch. –– çizelgesi roster (showing to whom shifts are assigned); mil. guard roster. –– gelmek to have a seizure or attack. –– hizmeti mil. guard duty. –– tutmak 1. to be on duty. 2. to stand guard; to keep watch, watch.
