gır

slang 1. word; talk; words. 2. made-up, fabricated, concocted. –– atmak slang to chat; to gossip. –– geçmek slang 1. to shoot the breeze, gab. 2. to pay no attention to one´s work, daydream. –– gıra almak /ı/ slang to make fun of. –– gır geçmek slang 1. /la/ to make fun of. 2. to waste time laughing and joking. –– kaynatmak slang to waste time laughing and joking.
