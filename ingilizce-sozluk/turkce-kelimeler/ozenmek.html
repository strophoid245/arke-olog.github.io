özenmek

1. /a/ to take pains (to do something, over something). 2. /a or dan/ to want to imitate (someone). 3. /a/ to try to (do something one knows little about): Şarkıcılığa özendi. She tried to become a singer. özene bezene very painstakingly, with the greatest of care. özene özene with utmost care, very painstakingly. özenip bezenmek to take great pains.
