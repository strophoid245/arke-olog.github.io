al

1. vermilion, flame scarlet, red. 2. bay (horse). 3. rouge. –– al crimson. ––ı al, moru mor flushed, purple in the face. –– basmak /ı/ to get puerperal fever. –– bayrak the Turkish flag. –– giymedim ki alınayım. colloq. Why should I take offense since I have done no such thing? ––lar giymek 1. to wear red. 2. to rejoice. –– kanlara boyanmak 1. to die a bloody death. 2. to be wounded. –– sancak the Turkish flag. –– yanaklı ruddy-cheeked.
