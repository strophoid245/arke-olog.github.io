pösteki

sheepskin or goatskin (with its wool or hair on it). ––ye dönmek (for something) to become battered and limp (from excessive use). –– saydırmak /a/ to have or make (someone) do a very boring, unending job. –– saymak to be engaged in a very boring, unending job. ––sini sermek/çıkarmak /ın/ to beat the living daylights out of (someone). ––yi sermek 1. (for a guest) to stay on and on with no apparent thought of leaving. 2. to hold on to one´s position as if one will never quit it.
