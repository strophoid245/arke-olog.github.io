kızak

1. sled; sledge; sleigh; toboggan; bobsled. 2. runner (of a sled, sleigh, or sledge). 3. skid. 4. naut. stocks, ways; ground ways; sliding ways. 5. mech. way. ––a çekmek /ı/ 1. to put (a ship) on the stocks. 2. colloq. to put (someone) into a position with less authority, put (someone) on the shelf; to kick (someone) upstairs. 3. to mothball, shelve, put (something) in cold storage. ––tan indirmek /ı/ to launch (a ship). –– yapmak (for a vehicle) to skid.
