arazi

1. piece of land; land. 2. open land, fields, or spaces. 3. lands; territory. –– açmak to clear land. –– arabası jeep. –– hukuku land law. –– olmak slang to get lost, beat it. –– sahibi landowner. –– tatbikatı mil. field exercise. ––ye uymak slang to lay low. –– vergisi land tax. –– vitesi gear for four-wheel drive.
