çaparız

obstacle, hitch, snag, difficulty. –– çıkmak for a problem to crop up, for something to go wrong. –– düşmek naut. (for one cable) to fall athwart (another). ––a gelmek to fall for it, be deceived. ––a getirmek /ı/ to trick, entrap (someone). –– vermek /a/ 1. to mess up, foul up, undo (something). 2. naut. to foul (anchors).
