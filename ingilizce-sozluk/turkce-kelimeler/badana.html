badana

1.whitewash. 2. latex paint; color wash; calcimine; distemper. –– etmek /ı/, –– vurmak /a/ 1. to whitewash. 2. to paint (something) with latex paint; to put a color wash on; to calcimine; to distemper. –– olmak 1. to be whitewashed. 2. to be painted with latex paint; to be painted with a color wash; to be calcimined; to be distempered.
