telef

1. loss of life, death. 2. waste, being wasted or squandered. 3. wasting away, going to ruin. –– etmek /ı/ 1. to kill, do away with. 2. to waste, throw away, squander. 3. to cause (someone, something) to waste away or go to ruin. –– olmak 1. to die, be done away with. 2. to be wasted, be thrown away, be squandered. 3. to waste away, go to ruin.
