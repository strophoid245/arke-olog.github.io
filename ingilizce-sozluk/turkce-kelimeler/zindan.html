zindan

1. hist. prison; close, dark prison, dungeon. 2. close, dark place; dungeon-like place. –– etmek /ı, a/ to make (a place) seem like a prison to, make (a place) a prison for, make (a place) a place of misery for. –– kesilmek 1. (for a place) to get very dark. 2. /a/ suddenly to become like a prison to (someone). –– olmak /a/ to become like a prison to, become a place of misery for (someone).
