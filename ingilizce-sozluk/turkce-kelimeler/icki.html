içki

1. alcoholic beverage, liquor, drink. 2. drinking, drinking alcoholic beverages. –– âlemi/sefası drinking party, carouse, carousal. –– bardağı glass made for serving an alcoholic drink. –– içmek/kullanmak to drink (habitually). –– sofrası/masası table spread for a drinking party. –– yasağı prohibition of alcoholic beverages.
