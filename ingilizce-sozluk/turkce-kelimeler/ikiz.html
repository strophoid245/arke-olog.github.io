ikiz

1. (a) twin; twin brother; twin sister. 2. (someone) born with someone else at the same birth, twin. Taşkın ikiz kardeşimdir. Taşkın´s my twin brother. Biz ikiziz. We´re twins. –– doğurmak 1. to give birth to twins. 2. colloq. to have a very hard time, have a devil of a time, have the devil´s own time. –– ev duplex, double house (two-family house divided vertically by a party wall).
