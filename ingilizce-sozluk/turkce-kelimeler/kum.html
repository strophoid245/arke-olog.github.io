kum

1. sand. 2. gravel (in the kidneys). 3. hard granules (in fruits such as pears or quinces). –– başı sandy beach. –– fırtınası sandstorm. –– gibi a great many. –– havuzu sandbox (for children). ––da oynamak colloq. to be left empty-handed (after having striven for something). –– saati hourglass. –– torbası sandbag.
