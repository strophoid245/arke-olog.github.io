alay

mockery, ridicule, teasing. ––a almak /ı/ to make fun (of), ridicule, laugh (at). –– etmek /la/ to make fun (of), ridicule, mock. –– geçmek /la/ slang to make fun (of). –– gibi gelmek /a/ to seem incredible (to). ––ında olmak /ın/ not to take (something) seriously, to take (something) as a joke.
