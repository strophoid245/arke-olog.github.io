fesat

1. disturbance, disorder, trouble. 2. corruption. 3. sedition. 4. always suspicious of others´ motives, distrustful. 5. (one) who stirs up trouble, mischief-making. –– başı chief troublemaker. –– karıştırmak/çıkarmak/kaynatmak to cause trouble, set people against each other. –– kumkuması a great mischief-maker. –– kurmak to conspire, plot. –– kutusu mischief-maker. –– ocağı/yuvası den of mischief. –– tohumu seeds of intrigue. ––a vermek /ı/ to stir up trouble in (a place).
