içkili

1. (someone) who has had something (alcoholic) to drink, who has had a drink or two. 2. (place, performance) at which liquor is served. 3. after having had a drink or two, with a drink or two under one´s belt: Nebahat´in o can sıkıcı partilerine ancak içkili gidilir. You can face those boring parties of Nebahat´s only if you´ve had a drink or two beforehand.
