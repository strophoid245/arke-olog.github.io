gıcık

1. tickle in the throat. 2. slang irritating, tiresome (person). –– almak /dan/, –– olmak /a/ to be irritated by (an action or a person). –– etmek /ı/ to get (one´s) goat, irritate. –– tutmak to have a tickle in one´s throat. –– vermek /a/ 1. (for something) to tickle (one´s) throat. 2. to arouse (one´s) jealousy.
