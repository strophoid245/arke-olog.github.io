iskele

1. quay, wharf, landing, pier, dock. 2. gangplank. 3. port town, port. 4. (a builder´s) scaffolding, scaffold. 5. the port side (of a ship). 6. cin. catwalk, mounting (for lights). –– almak 1. to hoist or remove the gangplank. 2. /a/ slang to pester (a woman) with improper advances. –– babası 1. bollard. 2. father who has no authority over his family. 3. henpecked husband. –– direği scaffolding pole. –– kurmak to erect scaffolding. –– vermek to lower the gangplank, put the gangplank in place.
