içerlek

1. (something) which sits back from (something else). 2. (something) which is located farther within (a place): Rıfkı hemen içerlek ve loş kütüphaneye koştu. Rıfkı ran at once into the dim library located farther within. 3. indented (line of writing, paragraph, etc.). 4. (placing something) closer to the inside, farther to the inside, farther within, more to the inside. –– yazmak /ı/ to indent (a line of writing, a paragraph. etc.).
