imla

1. spelling; orthography. 2. dictation. 3. filling up. ––sı bozuk (someone) who is bad at spelling; (something) that contains spelling errors. –– etmek /ı/ 1. to dictate. 2. to fill, fill up. ––ya gelmemek (for something) to be incorrigible, unsalvable, or irremediable. –– yanlışı spelling mistake.
