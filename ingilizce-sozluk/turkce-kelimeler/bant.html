bant

1. tape. 2. band. 3. hairband; headband. ––a almak /ı/ to record (something) on tape. –– doldurmak to tape, do the recording: Gelecek iki saat bant dolduracağız. We´re going to be taping for the next two hours. ––tan vermek /ı/ to broadcast (a radio program) from a tape recording; to broadcast (a television program) from a video tape.
