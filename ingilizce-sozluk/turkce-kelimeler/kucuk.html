küçük

1. little, small. 2. young, little. 3. petty, small, small-minded. 4. petty, minor, low-ranking. 5. miniature, small-scale. 6. petite, dainty. K––! Hey little one! (said to a child). –– aptes 1. the need to urinate. 2. urination.
