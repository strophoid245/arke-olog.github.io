bozuntu

1. a poor excuse for: şair bozuntusu a poor excuse for a poet. 2. (someone) who has the air of, who looks like (something undesirable): casus bozuntusu bir gazeteci a reporter who has the air of a spy. 3. ruins. ––ya uğramak to be completely dumbfounded, be stunned. ––ya vermemek to act as if nothing has happened, not to bat an eyelid.
