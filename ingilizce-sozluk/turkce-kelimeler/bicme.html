biçme

1. /ı/ cutting and shaping, hewing (wood, stone). 2. /ı/ tailor. cutting (cloth) (in order to make a garment); cutting out, cutting the cloth for (a garment). 3. /ı/ reaping (a cereal crop); cutting (hay, grass). 4. geom. prism. 5. hewn piece of stone; cut stone. 6. hewn wood, roughly squared wood. –– çayırı hayfield.
