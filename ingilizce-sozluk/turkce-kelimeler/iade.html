iade

1. sending back, returning, return. 2. restoration; restitution; reinstatement. 3. replying in kind to (a critical remark). 4. obs. repetition, repeating. –– etmek /ı/ 1. to send back, return. 2. to restore; to reinstate. 3. to reply to (a critical remark) in kind, retort. ––i itibar law reinstatement of rights (to a former bankrupt), rehabilitation. ––i ziyaret returning a visit.
