kavga

1. quarrel, row; brawl, fight. 2. struggle. –– aramak to look for trouble. –– bizim yorganın başına imiş. colloq. I was an onlooker who ended up getting hurt. –– çıkarmak to provoke a quarrel; to pick a fight. –– etmek /la/ to quarrel (with); to fight (with). –– kaşağısı person who incites others to quarrel. ––ya tutuşmak/girişmek to start quarreling (with each other).
