yenge

1. uncle´s wife, affinal aunt. 2. sister-in-law, brother´s wife. 3. used when referring informally to one´s own wife or to a friend´s wife: Yenge hanım nasıl? How´s your wife? Maalesef yengen gelemedi. Unfortunately my wife couldn´t come. 4. woman who helps and attends a bride. –– olmak slang 1. to give up hope. 2. to give up.
