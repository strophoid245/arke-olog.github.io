düşünce

1. thought, thinking. 2. idea, opinion, reflection, observation. 3. anxiety, worry. ––sini açmak to express one´s thought. –– alışverişi exchange of opinions. (bir) ––dir almak /ı/ to begin to worry, begin to worry about something. ––ye dalmak to be lost in thought. ––sini okumak /ın/ to read (someone´s) mind. –– özgürlüğü freedom of thought. ––ye varmak to reach an opinion.
