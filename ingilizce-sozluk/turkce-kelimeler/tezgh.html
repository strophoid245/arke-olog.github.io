tezgâh

1. counter (long table in a shop or office separating clientele and personnel and over which business transactions are made or food is served); (small, portable) display stand or case (used by street sellers). 2. counter (in a kitchen) 3. workbench. 4. loom (used for weaving). 5. stocks, ways, shipway. –– başı yapmak slang to stand at the bar and have a drink. ––tan geçirmek /ı/ slang to lay, have sex with. ––ından geçmek /ın/ slang (for a woman) to have sex with, go to bed with. –– kurmak slang to have sexual intercourse, have sex, do it. ––ı kurmak to set up shop, hang out one´s shingle.
