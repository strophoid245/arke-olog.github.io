dağılmak

1. to scatter, become scattered; to disperse; to dissipate. 2. (for a meeting) to break up; (for school) to let out; (for an association) to dissolve, come to an end. 3. to disintegrate, fall to pieces; to spall, spall off, spall away. 4. to become messy, get untidy. Dağılın! 1. mil. Dismissed! 2. police Break it up!
