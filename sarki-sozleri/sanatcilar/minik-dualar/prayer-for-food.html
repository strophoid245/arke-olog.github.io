Prayer For Food

Minik Dualar

BEFORE WE EAT WE SAY BISMILLAH
THAN WE SAY ELHAMDULILLAH

BEFORE WE START THE DINNER
MY FATHER SAID BISMILLAH
AFTER HE ATE HIS DINNER
HE SAID ELHAMDULILLAH

THAN I ASKED MY FATHER
WHAT DOES BISMILLAH MEAN

THEN I ASKED MY MOTHER
WHAT IS ELHAMDULILLAH

MY FATHER SAID DEAREST SON
ALLAH GAVE US FOOD TO EAT
BEFORE HAVING ANYTHING
WE ALWAYS MENTION ALLAHS NAME

AFTER FINISHING OUR MEAL
THANKS IS SAID FOR THE BLESSINGS
AFTER FINISHING OUR MEAL
WE SAY ELHAMDULILAH
